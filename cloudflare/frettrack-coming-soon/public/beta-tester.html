FretTrack beta testing checklist

Use this package to test the repair-shop workflow and report useful, reproducible feedback. Recommended: download the Excel workbook. It includes a compact checklist, detailed test references, and a bug log.

Download Excel Workbook CSV fallback Open Beta Login

Who should use it

Shop owners, admins, techs, viewers, and invited testers who are checking real FretTrack workflows on desktop, mobile, tablet, and older browser devices.

How to test

Work through the rows with realistic but non-private shop data. Record the role, browser/device, result, severity, notes, and screenshots when something breaks.

How to submit

Use the in-app Report Issue control when you have app access, or send completed checklists, screenshots, and recordings to support@frettrack-app.com.

Status and severity values

Status

  • Pass
  • Fail
  • Blocked
  • Not Tested
  • Needs Clarification

Severity

  • Low
  • Medium
  • High
  • Critical

Include with bugs

  • Browser and device
  • Shop role
  • Job, customer, part, vendor, or PO reference
  • Screenshot or video link
  • Whether it reproduces

Checklist sections

  • Login and Shop Access
  • New Job Intake
  • Job Detail and Workflow
  • Customers
  • Work Logs and Job History
  • Photos and Damage Map
  • Scheduling
  • Inventory Parts
  • Vendors, Purchase Orders, and Receiving
  • Barcode Labels
  • Reports / Advanced Reporting
  • Roles and Permissions
  • Offline / Draft Behavior
  • Mobile and Browser Testing
  • Final Feedback
